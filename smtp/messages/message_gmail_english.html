Project

Badkan is a website that brings together students and instructors in computer science and automatically corrects exercises prepared in advance by an instructor. Any instructor using Badkan will save correction time. Students receive immediate feedback (including a mark) after submitting their responses. All-in-one platform with many options that makes it easy for instructors and students.
